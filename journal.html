Why terroir matters for kokum, the same way it matters for wine.
Kokum from the Konkan coast contains 42–47% stearic acid by weight. The same Garcinia indica grown 200 km inland produces fruit with 31% stearic. The fatty acid profile is what makes kokum melt at skin temperature — change it, and you change the entire occlusive feel. This is why we won't accept "kokum from India." Only kokum from a 60-km strip of Maharashtra and Kerala coast.
