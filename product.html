N° 04 / The ritual

Three drops. AM and PM.

Apply 3–4 drops to clean, slightly damp skin. Press into face and neck. Follow with The Barrier Object (cream). Layer SPF in the AM. Begin every other day for the first week if your skin is sensitive — the bakuchiol is potent without being irritating, but introduce slowly.
